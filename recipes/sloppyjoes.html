Sloppy Joe's

Description

The best homemade Sloppy Joe's you'll ever have!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 can chicken gumbo soup, drained
  • Buns

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef, drain when finished
  2. Add the chicken gumbo soup, ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard
  3. Simmer covered for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Place heaping scoops onto the buns and enjoy!
