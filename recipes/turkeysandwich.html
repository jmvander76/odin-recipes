Turkey Sandwich

Description

It's a turkey sandwich, there's not much more to it

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce, cut into approximately bread size pieces
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Onion, sliced
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Put the ingredients on the bread
  2. Eat the sandwich
