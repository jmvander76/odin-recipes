Burritos

Description

Rice, bean, and sausage burritos, made camp style

Ingredients

  • 1 box Uncle Ben's Instant Rice
  • 1 package sausage (or kielbasa if you prefer)
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 package burrito seasoning
  • Burrito-sized tortilla shells
  • Your favorite toppings, such as sour cream, lettuce, tomato, taco sauce, etc

Directions

  1. Cook the Uncle Ben's Rice according to the package instructions
  2. While the rice is cooking, cook the sausage in a pan on the stovetop
  3. Combine the ingredients into the pot of rice on the stovetop, including the black beans
  4. Cook on low-medium until warmed through
  5. Fill tortilla shells, fill with your favorite toppings, and roll into burritos
